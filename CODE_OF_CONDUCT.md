# Code of Conduct

> "After all, we're all alike."
> — The Mentor, [*The Hacker Manifesto*](https://phrack.org/issues/7/3)

This Code of Conduct is loosely based on the
[cumbuca.dev Code of Conduct](https://github.com/cumbucadev/contributions/blob/main/CODE_OF_CONDUCT_EN.md)
(itself adapted from the [Contributor Covenant][homepage] 2.1), narrowed to
the scope of this single repository.

## Scope

This document governs participation in this repository: issues, pull requests,
discussions, code review comments, and any other interaction tied to this
project.

## Standards

Behavior that helps the project:

* Demonstrating empathy and kindness toward other people
* Being respectful of differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences
* Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback
* Accepting responsibility for mistakes and learning from them
* Focusing on what's best for the project and for the people working on it

Behavior that is not welcome here:

* Sexualized language or imagery, and sexual attention or advances of any kind
* Trolling, insulting or derogatory comments, and personal or political attacks
* Public or private harassment
* Publishing others' private information (physical address, email, etc.)
  without their explicit permission
* Other conduct that would be inappropriate in a professional setting

## Zero Tolerance

The Mentor wrote that we are all alike — curious, hungry, here for the work.
That premise is load-bearing. The following are not subject to warnings,
mediation, or "intent matters" debates. A single demonstrated instance is
sufficient grounds for a permanent ban from this repository:

* Ageism
* Homophobia
* Transphobia, including misgendering or deadnaming a person after being
  corrected
* Racism, including racial slurs, "jokes," or dog-whistles
* Xenophobia, including nationalist hostility and "go back to your country"
  rhetoric
* Ableism, including slurs targeting disability, neurodivergence, or mental
  health, and arguments that any class of people is intellectually inferior
* Misogyny and sex-based harassment
* Antisemitism, Islamophobia, and other forms of religious hatred
* Casteism
* Fatphobia and body-based harassment
* Advocacy of violence or dehumanization of any of the groups above

Disagreement on technical merit is welcome. Hostility toward who someone *is*
is not. There is no "playing devil's advocate" exemption.

## Enforcement

Maintainers are responsible for enforcing this Code of Conduct and may take
any action they consider appropriate — including editing or removing comments,
commits, issues, and pull requests; closing or locking threads; and banning
contributors from the repository.

Reports may be sent to the maintainer at
<9078708+niltonfrederico@users.noreply.github.com>. Reports will be reviewed
promptly. The reporter's identity will be kept confidential.

## Enforcement Ladder

Violations falling under [Zero Tolerance](#zero-tolerance) skip directly to
step 4.

1. **Correction** — private written warning explaining why the behavior was
   inappropriate. A public apology may be requested.
2. **Warning** — a warning with consequences for continued behavior. No
   interaction with the people involved, including those enforcing this
   document, for a specified period.
3. **Temporary Ban** — temporary ban from any interaction with the repository
   for a specified period. No public or private interaction with the people
   involved during this period.
4. **Permanent Ban** — permanent ban from any interaction with the repository.
   Applied for sustained patterns of violation, harassment of an individual,
   aggression toward classes of people, or any single Zero Tolerance violation.

The ladder is adapted from [Mozilla's code of conduct enforcement ladder][Mozilla CoC].
